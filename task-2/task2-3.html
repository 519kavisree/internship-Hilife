As part of this act, Ontario business owners with more than 50 employees, whether private or non-profit, schools, and organisations in the public sector must either create or refresh website content to meet WCAG 2.0 Level A – AA guidelines by January 1, 2021. Content is defined as any text, images, video, or sound.
